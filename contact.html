Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for Mount Kinabalu climbs and Sipadan diving permits which have limited availability.

Do you offer customized tours?

Yes! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel dates. Contact us with your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), PayPal, and cash payments at our office in Kota Kinabalu.
